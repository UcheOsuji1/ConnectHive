Simple Process

How ConnectHive Works

From profile to Hive in minutes. Five clear steps to finding your people.

1
Create Your Profile
Add your interests, skills, goals, personality, and availability. The more detail, the better your matches.
2
Choose Your Purpose
Select the type of Hive you want: social, professional, travel, project, event, or specialized.
3
Discover Compatible Hives
Browse recommended Hives with compatibility scores, descriptions, member counts, and tags.
4
Join or Create a Hive
Request to join an existing Hive or create a new one around your own goal. Don't see the right fit? Build it.
5
Connect Inside the Hive
Chat, plan events, share resources, collaborate, and build real connections with your group.
